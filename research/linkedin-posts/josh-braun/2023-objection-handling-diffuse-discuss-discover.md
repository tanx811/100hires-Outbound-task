# Diffuse → Discuss → Discover: staying calm under cold-call objections

A three-step pattern for handling pushback without sounding rattled.

**"How did you get my cell number?!"**
> Diffuse: "I'm using a tool called ZoomInfo that has your cell and office number. I'm sorry, it sounds like it's inappropriate for me to call your cell."

**"Just send me an email."**
> Discuss/Discover: "Sounds like there's something specific you'd like to see" — then ask permission before sending anything, instead of blasting irrelevant info.

**"Call me back later."**
> Diffuse: "I'm sorry, later?" — then discuss and discover the actual timing constraint before re-pitching the reason for the call.

Braun's point: staying calm and curious instead of defensive prevents the rep from feeling "off balance," and the skill is built through deliberate practice on talk tracks (he references 34 objection talk tracks in the comments).
