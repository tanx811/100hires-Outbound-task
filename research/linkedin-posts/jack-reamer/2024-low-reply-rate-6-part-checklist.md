# Low reply rate? Fix it with this 6-part checklist

Post directs readers to a Loom video walkthrough of Reamer's diagnostic process for broken outbound campaigns. Key timestamps called out:

- **4:32** — the one question to ask on a sales call that "drastically speeds up market research" for your ICP
- **5:55** — how to "10x your copywriting skills (and reply rate) in 60 minutes — even if you're allergic to sales or copywriting"

**Why it's here:** Reamer treats low reply rate as a *diagnosis* problem first, not a copywriting problem. His campaign-fix process mirrors SalesBread's internal audit flow for new client onboarding — fixes list quality and targeting before rewriting a single word of copy. That sequence (list → offer → copy) maps to Berman's 30/30/50 rule elsewhere in this research set.
