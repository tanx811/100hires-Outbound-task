# Gmail deliverability warning: don't reply to "unsubscribe" language

Florin flags a deliverability risk for SDR/outbound teams:

> "Gmail now penalizes you if you simply respond to an email where somebody mentions words like 'unsubscribe' or 'remove me from your list'."

He frames this as an emerging, underappreciated problem: Gmail/Outlook are tightening algorithmic spam enforcement, and teams that keep replying to opt-out language risk sender reputation damage — even when the reply itself is polite.

**Notable pushback in comments:** Ryan Lallier challenged the claim, asking for documented evidence from Google/Workspace, and noted he had successfully kept corresponding after a prospect replied "remove" with no penalty. Kept here for balance — the SDR community's own reaction shows this is contested, not settled, deliverability folklore.
