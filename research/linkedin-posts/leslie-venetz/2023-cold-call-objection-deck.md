# Free 52-slide Cold Call Objection Deck — and the 3C framework behind it

Venetz released a 52-slide deck with 40 sample replies across 13 of the most common early-call objections. Each objection gets 2–3 response options calibrated to different selling styles (direct, curious, empathetic).

**The underlying framework: 3C**
> Curiosity → Conversation → Conclusion

"A significant mindset shift": treat objections as a reason to get curious, not defensive. An objection is a signal that the prospect is still on the line and willing to say something — which means there is still a conversation to be had.

**Credibility anchor:** "WELL over 100k cold calls and has coached reps who've made millions" — this is the credential that makes the deck worth using, not just the volume of slides.

**Cross-reference:** this pairs directly with Braun's Diffuse → Discuss → Discover pattern (`research/linkedin-posts/josh-braun/`). Both treat objections as a discussion prompt, not a wall.
