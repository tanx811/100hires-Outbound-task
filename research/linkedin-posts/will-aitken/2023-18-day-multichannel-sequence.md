# The 18-day multi-channel cadence Will uses to refill pipeline

Will describes this sequence as one that "saved my ass multiple times when I realized my pipe was looking light." Built around Sales Navigator list-building, then:

| Day | Touch |
|---|---|
| 1 | Cold call voicemail + email |
| 3 | LinkedIn comment, LinkedIn connect, cold call |
| 5 | Video email (threaded as a reply, to dodge spam filters) |
| 7 | LinkedIn engagement + message |
| 9 | Email (his highest response-rate touch) |
| 10 | Cold call |
| 14 | LinkedIn voice note |
| 15 | Cold call voicemail |
| 18 | "Non-corny" breakup email |
| — | 3-month cooldown, then re-target other stakeholders at the account |

Templates/scripts referenced via a Vidyard guide titled "Sales Feed 18 Day Sprint Sequence Guide." The structural takeaway for a pipeline playbook: stack channels per touch (call + email + LinkedIn in the same week) rather than running single-channel sequences in isolation, and always have a defined breakup + cooldown step instead of outreach that just fades out.
