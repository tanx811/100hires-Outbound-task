# Alex Berman's cold email framework

**Core principle:** "Who you send an email campaign to is more important than the content of the email." Targeting beats copywriting.

**The 3C framework** for the email itself:
1. **Compliment** — open with a specific, researched detail (funding round, product launch, news mention).
2. **Case Study** — concrete proof: a measurable result from a similar company.
3. **Call to Action** — one low-friction ask, not a menu of options.

**The 30/30/50 rule** for where cold email success actually comes from:
- 30% targeting quality
- 30% offer design
- 50% deliverability / inbox placement
