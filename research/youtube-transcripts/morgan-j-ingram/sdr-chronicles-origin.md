# Why The SDR Chronicles exists

Ingram wrote this as an SDR at Terminus, before building the channel into his current AMP Social coaching practice:

> "Every day, the SDR is faced with multiple variations of rejection."

**The gap he identified:** new grads entering SDR roles had no practical exposure to cold calling, cold email, or social outreach before being thrown into quota — and no mentorship pipeline existed for the role specifically (as opposed to AE/closer content, which was already well covered).

**The fix:** a YouTube series (sdrchronicles.com companion site) built around tactical mentorship from practicing SDRs and sales leaders, rather than top-down management theory.

**Why this matters for the pipeline:** Ingram's content has since scaled into AMP Social, training reps at Google, Salesforce, Slack, and Snowflake on "sounding human" in outbound — directly relevant to the cold email/cold call quality problem other sources in this set (Braun, Tatulea) also flag.
