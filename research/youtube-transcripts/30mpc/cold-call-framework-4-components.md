# 30MPC's 4-part human-centered cold call framework

1. **Mindset** — shift from outcome anxiety ("book the meeting") to conversation curiosity. "The point of making cold calls is not to book meetings but to start conversations."
2. **Opening techniques** — two valid patterns: permission-based openers (respect the prospect's autonomy to end the call) and context-first openers (establish credibility via a known connection/trigger before self-introduction).
3. **Pitch structure** — break the pitch into segments instead of one monologue: status quo → why it's a problem → solution → proof (customer result) → check relevance. "Gives you the option to pivot" mid-call based on reaction.
4. **Vocal delivery** — smiling, standing, power posing, controlled breathing, deliberate pacing — mechanical levers for sounding confident rather than scripted.

Relevant because 30MPC is the highest-profile pure sales podcast putting out tactical, operator-level (not consultant-level) cold-call breakdowns weekly — a direct counterpart to the email/LinkedIn-heavy sources elsewhere in this set.
