# "RIP Cold Email: Here's What Works Now"

Dunning's solo episode argues that cold outreach's returns have been declining for B2B/SaaS specifically, and lays out what his agency is shifting client budget toward instead:

- **Why cold outreach is underperforming** in 2026 for B2B/SaaS, specifically — diminishing reply rates as inboxes get noisier and filters get stricter (echoes the deliverability concerns raised by Tatulea and Reamer elsewhere in this research set).
- **Strategic pivot** toward organic/inbound lead generation as a complement, not full replacement, for outbound.
- **AI search visibility** — optimizing for being surfaced in AI-assisted search/answer engines as a new top-of-funnel channel.
- **Bottom-of-funnel SEO** prioritized over broad/top-of-funnel content, on the logic that buyer intent matters more than traffic volume.

Included as the dissenting/balancing voice in this set of 10: a practitioner who runs outbound-adjacent lead gen and is willing to publicly say where cold outreach alone is no longer enough — useful tension for a real playbook instead of one-sided cheerleading.
