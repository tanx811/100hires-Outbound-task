# LinkedIn outreach strategy — SalesBread's 6-step framework

SalesBread runs LinkedIn outreach campaigns for B2B clients and claims up to a 39% positive reply rate. The framework:

1. **Profile optimization** — treat your profile as "a sales page," not a resume: recent photo, headline that states the value you deliver.
2. **Prospect list building** — data-driven ICP filtering (industry, location, company size) before any outreach goes out.
3. **Personalized messaging** — the **CCQ method**: open with a Compliment, Commonality, or Question specific to the prospect.
4. **Strategic follow-ups** — a Fibonacci-spaced follow-up cadence; reply rate per touch declines but cumulative reply rate keeps climbing.
5. **Low-commitment CTAs** — ask for a short call, not a big commitment, in the first ask.
6. **Measurement** — track connection acceptance rate (25–40% healthy) and reply rate (20%+ target) as the two core health metrics.

**Tactical rules:** keep messages to 2–3 sentences max; apply the "90/10 principle" — 90% of the message should be about the prospect, 10% about you. Reamer cites LinkedIn driving "277% more leads than Facebook and Twitter" for B2B.

Relevant because SalesBread's whole business is volume LinkedIn outreach for SaaS clients — this is the agency's own production process, not a generic guide.
